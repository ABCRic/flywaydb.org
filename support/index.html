Support

If you're having trouble with Flyway, the Flyway developers and the Flyway community are here to help you!

Questions

If you have doubts or if you are stuck, you can ask a question under the flyway tag on StackOverflow.

Features and Issues

If you think you've found an issue or if you would like to suggest a feature, you can do so in the Issue Tracker.

We usually focus on fixing issues first, and then come new features.

If there is a feature or a fix your organisation wants, and it hasn't been implemented yet, you also have the option to pay a bounty to have it implemented sooner.

Training

We also offer professional training to quickly get you up to speed and teach you all there is to know about Effective Database Migrations with Flyway.
